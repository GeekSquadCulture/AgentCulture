  • BRANCHES OF SERVICE

    As our client’s and our business have evolved over the years, so too have the branches of Geek Squad. Today, the functions that our Geek Squad Agents perform fall into the following branches of service.

    ALTHOUGH EACH ROLE DIFFERS, THEIR MISSION REMAINS THE SAME.

    BRANCH: IN STORE
    TITLE: COUNTER INTELLIGENCE AGENT (CIA)
    CIAs police technology from within the Geek Squad Precinct. Services include:

    • Computer setup and repair
    • Smartphone setup and repair
    • Tablet setup and repair

    BRANCH: CONNECTED HOME
    TITLE: FIELD AGENT
    Field Agents police technology in our client’s home. Services include:

    • Networking
    • Computer Setup and Repair
    • Audio / Video setup and Installation
    • Smart Home
    • TV Calibration

    BRANCH: AUTOTECH
    TITLE: AUTOTECH
    Autotechs police technology in their client's vehicles from within the Installation Bay.

    Their work includes(but is not limited to) installing the following products:

    • Alarm/Security
    • Remote starter
    • Decks
    • Audio/Video
    • GPS

    BRANCH: ONLINE SUPPORT
    TITLE: COVERT OPERATIONS AGENT
    Covert Operations Agents police technology from undisclosed locations across the country and are capable of completing a wide range of tasks to support our clients. This includes:

    • Computer, smartphone and tablet setup
    • Tutorials and support
    • Troubleshooting and software repair
    • Networking
    • Dating Advice
